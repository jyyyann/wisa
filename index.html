Wellington International Students' Association

Welcome to WISA

Wellington International Student Association is founded in 2019. We are a platform that connects international students in the Wellington region. Our vision for this club is to allow the opportunity for international students to make the most of, but more importantly, enjoy their time studying in Wellington. We organise events and hangouts which allow students to meet up regularly and have fun while meeting new people from all around the globe.
