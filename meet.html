WISA Executive team

  Media & Promotion

  • Managing social media including Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter
  • Designing posters and Instagram posts on upcoming events
  • Responding to messages and inquiries on social media
  • Publishing and promoting Merchandise

  Event Coordination

  • Advocating ideas for events
  • Planning event details and aspects, including seating, catering and guests
  • Writing run-downs and assigning jobs to other executive members
  • Ensuring everything runs smoothly during the events
  • Fulfilling the role of MC during events

  Treasury

  • Managing funding, fundraising and sales
  • Financial planning and budgeting
  • Financial reporting during executive meetings
  • Maintain records by tracking every income and expense financial transaction
  • Booking and purchasing equipment, food, and prizes for individual events

  Secretary

  • Preparing the agenda for executive meetings
  • Making arrangements including venue, date, times and hospitality for club meetings
  • Minute-taking during executive meetings
  • Contact external parties on behalf of WISA
