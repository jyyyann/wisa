2021 events


Movie Fundraiser

We collaborated with the Interact Club of Port Nicholson Volunteering Club for a Movie Night Fundraiser for Blue Children's Dragon Foundation, a charity in Vietnam that helps refugees and rescues children from slavery. It is a tradition that WISA collaborates with Interact club for an annual fundraising event. The Marvel movie Shang Chi was played. Over 60 people attended the event. We raised $500, which was all donated to the charity.


14 Nov 2021

Penthouse Cinema

Movie Night

We organised a movie night event at the AGC Hall at Wellington College. The movie Weathering with You was played. We ordered pizza for catering. Cup-sized popcorn and soda drinks were also prepared for our participants. Over 100 students attended the event.


10 Jul 2021

Wellington College

6:00pm - 9:00pm

$5.00 NZD

Roller Skating Night

We hired a court at the Kilbirnie Recreation Centre for the roller skating event. We divided our participants into two teams and arranged activities, including a tag game, roller skate hockey, and relay race. Over 60 students attended the event, including both international and domestic students.


4 Jun 2021

Kilbirnie Recreation Centre

7:30pm - 9:30pm

$8.00 NZD

Bowling Night

We hired 7 lanes at The Lanes for our bowling event. We randomised teams, so everyone had an opportunity to socialise with students from other schools. We organised a "bowling league" where the winning team got a prize. A total of 42 students participated in the event.

Sports Day

We organised the first event of the year - a sports day at Wellington College. We divided our participants into teams for competitions such as football matches and tug of war. We then served hot dogs for afternoon tea. The event was followed by an election for the WISA 2021 Executive Committee, where all candidates gave their speeches.
