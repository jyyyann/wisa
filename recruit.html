2022 WISA Executive

With most of the WISA 2021 executive members heading to University, we need people to fill their roles so that WISA can keep running in 2022. Whether or not you are an international student, we welcome you to the family of our organization!


Listed below are the job vacancies and description, if any of them sounds like you, scroll down to the bottom of the page and nominate yourself via the Google form before 10th Feb!

We are looking for...

Graphic Designer

Are you a Digital Design Technology student? Do you have an Adobe account? Do you have experience in designing logo, poster, or merchandise? If so, this role is perfect for you!


The duties of a Graphic Designer include:

‣ Designing Instagram posts and posters

‣ Designing Merchandise



Secretary

Can you communicate effectively? Are you a well organised person who can delegate tasks? Can you maintain confidentiality on relevant matters? If this is you, apply now to be the Secretary of WISA 2022!


The duties of a Secretary include:

‣ Preparing the agenda for executive meetings in consultation with the President

‣ Making arrangements including venue, date, times and hospitality for club meetings

‣ Minute-taking during executive meetings

‣ Replying to emails and DMs promptly on behalf of WISA

Why be a part of WISA 2022?

1. Gain experience

Undoubtedly, holding leadership positions look good on a résumé. Whether you are applying for scholarship, internship, or a job in the future, having led a student organization shows that you’re hard working and can handle multiple responsibilities. Throughout the course of the year, you will learn a lot about event planning, financial budgeting, managing social media, contacting sponsorship, and more!

2. Build your network

WISA is the largest umbrella body that gather international students in the Wellington region. You will have the opportunity to connect with students from schools and countries other than your own. By working closely with each other, you'll build lifelong friendship with your fellow executive members. We have connection with Wellington NZ and Education NZ. We also collarboarte with the Interact Portnic Club every year.

3. Have fun!

While being a part of the executive team means a lot of dedication and committment, you will have a lot of fun working in a team. WISA gives you the platform to pitch and execute ideas you're passionate about, and back you up with manpower, resources, and budget. You will gain a great sense of accomplishment when you see the smiley faces on our event attendees. We also host exec-only gatherings from time to time in recognition of our hardwork.
