About WISA

Our Vision


Support

With the possibility of border reopening next year, we might be welcoming more international students to Wellington. We have first hand experience how tricky it could be to settle in a new country on our own, we hope to support new students integrating to the community more seamlessly.

Culture

We hope to organise more cultural-focused events this year and encourage our participants to learn about each other's culture. Initial plans are cultural festival with food booths, game booths and movies.

Welfare

We hope to organise more cultural-focused events this year and encourage our participants to learn about each other's culture. Initial plans are cultural festival with food booths, game booths and movies.

Contribute

Volunteering allows international students assimilate and become a contributing members of Wellington's Community. WISA is planning on registering our members as a SVA (Student Volunteering Army) Volunteering Team. Not only does the volunteering hours count towards a regular SVA pins, but also a special awards that only members of the team can earn.

Community

We aim to build a stronger bond within the WISA community through engaging more with our members. We hope to host events more regularly and frequently. We will also act as the channel that pass on first hand information on interesting events, leadership oppotunities, or even job opportunities.
